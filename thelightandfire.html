You're the Light and Fire















This is an atmospheric music video for a song that I wrote. I used a lower framerate funnled through a higher framerate, to give the clip an aged, antique feel. Also, I hope you like the song. This song is supposed to communicate loneliness, longing and small optimism. I wanted this to feel like it was almost found footage.


This was a little more difficult than I thought. I ran into several issues: First, my computer can barely run after effects. Second, I don't like how i look when I lipsync. Third, I have missing keys on the keyboard, so certain hotkeys won't work. I wasn't able to splice how I wanted to, so I reshot the video as is, as one whole piece.
