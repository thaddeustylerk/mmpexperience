Declan's abilities, were surprisingly the only aspect of himself and his history that he was willing to divulge freely. Below is the transcript from when Declan explained the extent of his abilities.


Hawthorne : Could you explain your... we'll call them "powers", can you explain them more?
Kent : (pause) Sure.
Kent : (drinks water, sets on table)
Kent : I've never said it out loud before. When I make up a melody, you know, when I write something? If I do it a certain way, and I sing it, it has sort of an affect on people.
Hawthorne : Could you be more specific?
Declan : All of us can sort of do this. Where I'm from, I mean. We can all do this when we sing, or play, or perform. Mine is a little more rare. When I play in front of a group, it's like I spill a vat of bottled luck. People are healed, people fall in love, people confess to crimes. It's different almost every time. I don't have much control over it.
Hawthorne : (pause)
Hawthorne : You mentioned that you can alter your appearance. Could you elaborate?
Kent : It's not like that. I can't change how I look any more than you, beyond a haircut. But I can change how you see me. It won't make sense to you. But I can make myself muted, forgettable. I can also make myself unforgettable, but not in the way that you think. People don't like that one.
Hawthorne : Could you show us the latter?
Kent :You sure?(pause)(a sound like metal wire stretching)
Hawthorne : (sharp breath)
Hawthorne : (looking down, but addressing Kent)Turn it off.
End Recording
